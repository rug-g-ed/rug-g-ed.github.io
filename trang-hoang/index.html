Yen Trang Hoang

PhD Candidate | Junker Lab

As a PhD candidate I aim to resolve how spatial context and clonal dynamics contribute to neuroblastoma transcriptional heterogeneity using a zebrafish tumor model. This work uniquely combines spatial and lineage-resolved data, providing a robust framework to study the regulation of cell state plasticity, identify potential therapeutic vulnerabilities, reveal principles of tumor evolution, and extend its application beyond neuroblastoma.
